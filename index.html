Sunway Team for Robocon 2023 is recruiting!

Are you interested in robotics? Are you someone who likes to do hands-on technical work? Do you want to learn more? You might be the one we are looking for!

What is ABU Robocon? mdi-wikipedia The ABU Asia-Pasific Robot Contest (ABU Robocon) is an Asian-Oceanian college robot competition, founded in 2002 by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union. Each team builds multiple robots to compete with each other by completing tasks specific to the theme of that year. Most countries have their own national competition to elect the representative teams to participate in the international competition. Robocon Malaysia mdi-web mdi-facebook Every year the Ministry of Higher Education Malaysia organises the national competition of Robocon to elect the teams to represent Malaysia. Sunway Robocon Team: A little of our history Sunway University has participated in Robocon Malaysia in year 2020 and 2022. Robocon Malaysia 2020 was concluded with just the submission of technical reports. We have travelled to USM Penang for the physical competition of Robocon Malaysia 2022. What are we looking for? We are looking for people who are interested with robotics. You may or may not have technical backgrounds, but willing to pick up hands-on skills to contribute to the robots building process. We will conduct technical workshops to help you to improve on your skills. What can you look forward to? You will involve in the design of the robots from the aspects of programming logic, robot mechanisms, interfacing between the mechanism with software control through electronics, etc. You do not have to involve in all of them, but most likely you will need to work in at least one of those aspects.
